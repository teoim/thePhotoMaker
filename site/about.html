Who he is

The Photo Maker aka Teodor I. Mitrache

IT student and passionate photographer

Rather than an art form, he perceives photography as a form of meditation and a way to better connect to the surrounding environment.

He got into photography some years ago, by chance. While he does shoot different kinds of Events, Street and Portraiture, his favourite cup of coffee is Landscape photography. He also enjoys shooting his meal occasionally ;-)

Good people, good food, good wine and good art are the main ingredients to a good life.
